About Liv

My name is Olivia and I’m a front-end developer with a love for building, responsive, and user-friendly web applications. My journey into tech started with pure curiosity—I’ve always been fascinated by how websites work, and once I started coding, I was fascinated. Before transitioning into development, I worked as an online English tutor and an AI Data Specialist.

Teaching helped me develop strong communication skills, while my work in AI taught me to think analytically and pay attention to detail—both of which come in handy when coding. So far, I’ve built a few projects, including two weather apps (one in JavaScript and one in React), as well as other responsive web applications using Bootstrap. I’m currently studying at SheCodes.

Outside of coding, I’m an introvert who enjoys quiet, creative hobbies like playing chess, reading, listening to music, and gardening. I also love traveling and unwinding with a good series. I’m excited to grow in the tech industry and keep building things that make an impact.
